A VR training simulator game designed for the Saskatchewan Mining Association. This game tasks the player with learning and following proper fire extinguisher technique in order to extinguish a class A, B, and C fire. Forklift VR

A VR training simulator game designed for Saskatchewan Polytechnic. This game tasks players with a series of trainging levels to test their ability to drive a forklift. This game also features custom 1-to-1 alignment of all the physical controls to provide the players with the most immersion possible.

Confined Spaces VR

A two part touch table project developed for the Saskatchewan Sports Hall of Fame. The first part is a large touch table database with information, photos, videos, and biographies of all the inductees into the Hall of Fame.
