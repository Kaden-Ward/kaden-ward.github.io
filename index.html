Grease Monster VR

A VR game developed for the City of Regina. This game tasks the player with cleaning the city's pipes and destroying the grease monster! Developed to help educate children about not putting grease down the drain.

Fire Extinguisher VR

A VR training simulator game designed for the Saskatchewan Mining Association. This game tasks the player with learning and following proper fire extinguisher technique in order to extinguish a class A, B, and C fire. Developed to assist with the Sask Mining Association fire extinguisher training program.

Forklift VR

A VR training simulator game designed for Saskatchewan Polytechnic. This game tasks players with a series of trainging levels to test their ability to drive a forklift. This game also features custom 1-to-1 alignment of all the physical controls to provide the players with the most immersion possible.

Confined Spaces VR

This training application helps train workers to experience confined spaces where users must inspect the welding seams of the vessel, and inspect posts in the crawlspace. Option for players to crawl in full room scale for greater immersion.

VR Training Simulator

Sask Sports Hall of Fame Virtual Display Case

A two part touch table project developed for the Saskatchewan Sports Hall of Fame. The first part is a large touch table database with information, photos, videos, and biographies of all the inductees into the Hall of Fame. The second part is a 4x touch screen monitor and webcam display case that shows selected artifacts from the first table in a psudo AR environment.
